The narrator's voice samples - both clips read the same sentence,
so what you hear is the difference between the tiers, nothing else.

  kokoro.mp3      tier 1 - Kokoro - runs great on any CPU
                  clear but flat: a capable reader, not an actor

  qwen-1.7b.mp3   tier 2 - Qwen, "the good voice" - needs a GPU (~2 GB)
                  expressive, takes pace instructions
                  (arrives in a later update)

Double-click a clip to hear it, then pick your tier when setup.bat asks.
You can change your mind anytime by running setup.bat again.
